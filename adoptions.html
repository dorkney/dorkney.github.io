WANT TO ADOPT?

Adoption applications must be submitted in person at our shelter. Adoption fee is $200 for cats 6 months or older and $225 for kittens less than 6 months. Kittens carry a higher cost because we give them everything they need to get off to healthy start. UNFORTUNATELY WE CANNOT ACCEPT CREDIT CARDS FOR ADOPTION FEES.

Upon intake, all animals are given a preliminary exam of the eyes, ears, mouth, heart, and lungs, and we check their body temperature.

We test ALL our cats and kittens using the triple combo test, which includes tests for leukemia, FIV, and heartworm; however, any kitten under 6 months old that tests negative, could, test positive after 6 months. Is it unlikely, BUT we do disclose this fact for you. Most kittens will NOT show a result for FIV or heartworm less than 6 months of age.

Why does it cost so much?

An adult cat (over 6 months) recieves the following:

  1. Microchipping
  2. Spay (female) or neuter (male)
  3. Testing for:
    • Heartworms
    • Feline leukemia
    • Feline FIV
  4. Treatment for:
    • Roundworms
    • Tapeworms
    • Earmites
    • Fleas
  5. Vaccines for:
    • Distemper (2 total treatments)
    • Rabies
  6. A fecal exam
  7. A take-home goodie bag with food, toys, and all medical records

A kitten (under 6 months) recieves the following:

  1. Microchipping
  2. Spay (female) or neuter (male)
  3. A fecal exam
  4. Testing for:
    • Heartworms
    • Feline leukemia
    • Feline FIV
  5. Treatment for:
    • Coccidia (intestinal parasites)
    • Giardia (intestinal parasites)
    • Roundworms
    • Tapeworms (if found in fecal exam)
    • Earmites
    • Fleas
  6. Vaccines for:
    • Distemper (4 total treatments)
    • Rabies
  7. A take-home goodie bag with food, toys, and all medical records

Because we deal with the abused and abandoned, many of our kitties receive much more than the above.

Applications must be submitted in person!
